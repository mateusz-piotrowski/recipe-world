Magic Cake

Magic Cake is one of the tastiest cake the world has known. Few have tried it and lived to tell about it, and these are their stories.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter

Directions

  1. Mix eggs, sugar, and butter in a large bowl
  2. Spread into a non-stick dish
  3. Bake at 350 degress for 1 hour
  4. Let sit at room temperature for 20 minutes
  5. Eat and enjoy!
